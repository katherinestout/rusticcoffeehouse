About Us

Rustic Coffeehouse was founded by Joe Rustic, while he was camping on the Appliachian trail back in 1989. Joe was on that trail for ten years before he made it out. What sustained him for that long by himself was the coffee he invented. From the rare beans of the Appliachia tree, he created a coffee like no other. You no longer need food, just coffee.

Our Mission

Our mission at Rustic Coffeehouse is to offer you a unique experience in your everyday life. There is no coffee like Rustic Coffeehouse coffee. We strive to be vegan gluten free friendly and organic without plastic straws. We are no starbucks.
