About Us

Rustic Coffeehouse was founded by Joe Rustic, while he was camping on the Appliachian trail back in 1989. Joe was on that trail for ten years before he made it out. What sustained him for that long by himself was the coffee he invented. From the rare beans of the Appliachia tree, he created a coffee like no other. You no longer need food, just coffee.

Our Mission

Our mission at Rustic Coffeehouse is to offer you a unique experience in your everyday life. There is no coffee like Rustic Coffeehouse coffee. We strive to be vegan gluten free friendly and organic without plastic straws. We are no starbucks.

Jammin Java

A light roast exotic blend of javascript and jquery beans. From the distant hills of html and css, you cant taste the tears of the coding bootcamp when they try to learn things. Or at least mine.

API Appliachia

The API Appliachia blend is slightly easier to take in then the Java, with it's robust flavor and slightly odd aftertaste this is a coffee unique like no other. The simplicity makes it beautiful.

Rustic Roast

Rustic Roast is Joe's first and original blend of coffee. It sustained him on the Appliachian trail for months, with little to no food. What makes this blend unique is how filling it is. Joe created the first coffee that can sustain coders and procrastinating college kids alike for multiple all nighters. Who needs food.
